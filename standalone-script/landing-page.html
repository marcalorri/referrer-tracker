Frequently Asked Questions

How does the tracking work?
Our JavaScript automatically captures UTM parameters, click IDs (gclid, fbclid, etc.), and referrer information from the URL and stores them in 1st-party cookies. When a visitor fills out a form, these values are automatically populated in hidden fields.
Do I need to modify my backend?
No! Referrer Tracker works entirely on the frontend. Just add the script to your website and the tracking data will be included in your form submissions automatically.
Is it GDPR compliant?
Yes! All cookies are 1st-party (set on your domain) and only store marketing attribution data. No personal information is collected by our script.
What click IDs do you support?
We support Google Ads (gclid, wbraid, gbraid, dclid), Facebook Ads (fbclid, fb_click_id, fbadid), Microsoft Ads (msclkid, msclid), and TikTok Ads (ttclid, ttclid_ss, clickid).
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
Do you offer a free trial?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we'll refund your payment in full.
